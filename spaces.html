Our Spaces

Our spaces are thoughtfully designed to foster creativity, collaboration, and comfort—ideal for team-building art workshops of any size. From bright, open studios with natural light and high ceilings to intimate nooks for focused group sessions, each space is fully equipped with supplies and flexible layouts.

Our art spaces

Line Drawing • Calligraphy • Murals

The Line Loft

The Line Loft is a focused, light-filled space where creativity begins with a single stroke. Perfect for workshops in line drawing, calligraphy, and large-scale murals, this studio invites teams to explore rhythm, form, and expression through mark-making. Whether you’re sketching portraits or co-creating a mural, The Line Loft offers a calm, inspiring setting to connect through precision, flow, and shared creativity.

Paint Jam • Puzzle Collage

The Color Lab

Bold, energetic, and joyfully messy, The Color Lab is where color comes to life. Designed for paint jams and puzzle collage workshops, this studio encourages teams to play with texture, layering, and expressive freedom. It’s a space where experimentation meets collaboration, and participants leave not only with vibrant artwork, but also a renewed creative spark and stronger team bonds.

Mixed Media Collage • Inspiration Studio

The Nest

The Nest is a cozy, flexible studio designed for reflection and imagination. With a focus on mixed media collage and inspiration-based workshops, it’s the perfect environment for teams to slow down, share ideas, and co-create meaningful pieces. Whether you’re layering images or building a concept board, The Nest nurtures thoughtful dialogue and creative exploration in a supportive, welcoming space.

The Commons is the heart of the studio—a vibrant, inclusive space open to all.
